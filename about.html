Introduction to Computer Science

Jump into our Intro to Computer Science class and get ready to explore the exciting world of technology :p This course is designed for beginners, so don’t worry if you have no prior experience. You’ll learn the basics of programming using popular languages like Python and Java. We'll cover everything from writing simple code to understanding how algorithms work and why they matter.

Throughout the course, you’ll tackle real-world problems and learn how to approach them with a logical mindset.

We’ll also touch on important topics like ethics in tech and the societal impact of computing. Whether you want to kickstart a career in tech, create your own projects, or simply gain a better understanding of the digital world, this class is a great place to start.
